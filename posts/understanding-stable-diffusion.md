Stable Diffusion is a text-to-image machine learning model developed by CompVis, Stability AI, and LAION. It's primarily used to generate detailed images conditioned on text descriptions, though it can also be applied to other tasks such as inpainting, outpainting, and generating image-to-image translations guided by a text prompt.

## How it works
Unlike traditional diffusion models, stable diffusion operates in the latent space of a pre-trained autoencoder. This makes it significantly faster and less computationally expensive.
