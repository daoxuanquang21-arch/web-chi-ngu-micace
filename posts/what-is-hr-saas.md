HR SaaS (Software as a Service) refers to human resources management software that is hosted in the cloud. Instead of installing software on local servers, companies access the application via a web browser.

## Benefits of HR SaaS
1. **Cost-Effective:** Lower upfront costs.
2. **Scalability:** Easily add new users as the company grows.
3. **Accessibility:** Access data from anywhere, anytime.

Cloud computing has revolutionized many industries, and HR is no exception. Modern platforms provide comprehensive suites covering payroll, recruitment, and performance management.
